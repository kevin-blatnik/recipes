Italian Chicken Breast

Description

Delicious and healthy browned chicken breast tenders

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs chicken breast
  • Italian dressing
  • Oil

Directions

  1. Slice chicken breast into small tenders
  2. Place tenders in Italian dressing overnight or at least 30 min
  3. Take tenders out of liquid onto bowl
  4. Heat pan on medium heat
  5. Cook tenders for about 5-10 minutes on each side
  6. Check tender with thermometer for 165 degrees
