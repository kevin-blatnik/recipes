Shrimp Tempura

Description

Delicious crispy fried shrimp with tempura sauce. The tempura batter may be used for other recipes. Try it with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 box tempura batter
  • 1 lb frozen shrimp
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 quarts oil

Directions

  1. Take out frozen shrimp half hour before starting
  2. Place shrimp in medium bowl with some ice
  3. Scrape shrimp tail with butter knife
  4. Squish each shrimp with 2 thumbs to break back
  5. Heat oil on med high heat
  6. Dip shrimp in egg yolk, then dip in flour/tempura batter mixture
  7. Slowly lay shrimp in oil. Cook each side up for a minute
  8. Remove and place on drying rack
