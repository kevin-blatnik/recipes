Shrimp Tempura

Description

Delicious, healthy egg white fried rice with mushrooms and spinach

Ingredients

  • 120 grams egg whites
  • 1/4 cup brown rice
  • 3 medium baby bella mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup baby spinach
  • 1 tablespoon heavy whipping cream
  • olive oil

Directions

  1. Place egg whites and heavy whipping cream in bowl and beat
  2. Heat non stick pan on medium heat
  3. Cook sliced mushrooms for a few minutes on each side
  4. Add spinach and rice for last 2 minutes
  5. Plate rice and vegetables, spray pan with oil
  6. Pour in egg whites and season with Everything Bagel Seasoning
  7. Mix often and plate after a few minutes
  8. Top with diced shallots, green cholula, and jalapeno salad topper:w
